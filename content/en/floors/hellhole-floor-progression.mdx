The core of this Roblox PvE horror shooter revolves around the **hellhole floor progression** system. To survive and escape, you must descend floor by floor into a dark, demonic underworld. As you descend deeper, the environment becomes progressively darker, requiring better light management, and the enemy spawns become far more aggressive. Understanding how these floors scale in difficulty is the first step toward reaching the deepest zones of the underworld.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Enemy Behaviors and Floor Threat Levels">

The creatures inhabiting the depths of the Hellhole are diverse and deadly. As you progress, you will encounter different classes of enemies, each requiring a specific tactical response. 

In the early floors, you will mostly face basic hostile creatures. These enemies rely on simple melee rushes and swarming tactics. While easy to dispatch individually, they can quickly overwhelm you if you get trapped in a corner. As the floor progression advances to the mid-tier floors, aggressive demon enemies begin to spawn. These demons possess higher health pools, deal devastating damage, and often utilize unpredictable movement patterns to close the distance. Finally, the deep floors introduce environmental floor threats and powerful boss encounters that require precise positioning and coordinated teamwork.

| Enemy Type | Primary Behavior | Combat Strategy | Threat Level |
| Hostile Creatures | Melee rush, swarming | Keep distance, use high-capacity weapons | Low |
| Demon Enemies | Aggressive pursuit, high damage | Dodge attacks, target head/weak spots | Medium |
| Floor Threats | Environmental traps, ambush | Move slowly, check corners and ceilings | High |

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Advantage">
Aim for critical weak spots on demon enemies to conserve ammunition and eliminate threats twice as fast.
</Callout>

</Section>
