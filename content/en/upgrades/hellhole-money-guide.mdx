<Section icon={Zap} title="Step-by-Step Farming Run">

Follow this optimized route to maximize your currency gain in every run. This process ensures you reach the boss with enough power and money to succeed.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Preparation">
    Equip the **Recon Optics** perk to see enemy counts and the **Start with 150 coins** perk. This allows you to buy your first upgrade at the very first shop encountered.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Floor 1-3 Looting">
    Focus on clearing every room. Look for the **Treasure Room** symbols on doors. Open every silver chest to build your initial bankroll of roughly 300-500 coins.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Mid-Run Shopping">
    Once you reach the Sunken Ruins or Fungal Depths, look for a Shop. Prioritize **Move Speed** and **Max HP**. Speed is essential for dodging projectiles from Spitters and Gremlins.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade Selection">
    Choose upgrades that increase your clearing speed. **Crit Damage** and **Reload Speed** are top-tier. The faster you kill, the less damage you take, saving you coins on healing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Boss Execution">
    Defeat the boss at floor 7. If solo, the boss has reduced health. Stay mobile to avoid poison pools. Collect the massive Gear drop and decide whether to descend further or extract.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The **Reload Heals** perk (unlocks early) provides 5 HP every time you finish a reload. This effectively gives you infinite sustain if you play carefully, allowing you to spend coins on Damage instead of Healing.
