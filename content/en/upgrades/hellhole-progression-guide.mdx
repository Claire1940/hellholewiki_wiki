<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat and PvP Management">

In a ganking hellhole, every alliance or horde player you see is a potential threat. Combat in 2026 is fast-paced, and even a few levels of difference can be bridged with skillful use of crowd control and environment. If you are playing a class like a Rogue, your stereotype as "scumish" behavior is actually your greatest asset.

When engaging in world PvP during your progression:
- **Use the Environment**: Lead enemies into high-density mob areas.
- **Cooldown Management**: Never engage a player if your main defensive (like Vanish or Bubble) is on cooldown.
- **Psychological Warfare**: Sometimes staying in stealth and just "watching" an enemy is enough to make them leave the area out of paranoia.

<Callout type="info" title="Honorable Kills">
