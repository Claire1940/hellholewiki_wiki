Early bosses often rely on a predictable loop. They will attempt to close the gap with a charge, followed by a three-hit combo. Once the combo concludes, the boss enters a "Vulnerable" state for approximately 3 seconds. This is your window to unleash your strongest attacks.

| Boss Phase | Behavior Pattern | Player Response |

| **Phase 2 (50-0%)** | Faster attacks, adds minions | Focus minions first, then boss |

<Section icon={Map} title="Deep Floor Challenges: Surviving the Abyss">

As you descend deeper into Hellhole, the challenges shift from simple combat to complex survival puzzles. Deep floor bosses introduce environmental mechanics, such as floor tiles that turn into lava or darkness clouds that obscure your vision. These encounters demand a high level of "Deep Run Preparation."

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Scout the Arena">
    Before the boss spawns, identify "Safe Zones" that are free from environmental hazards. These are usually elevated platforms or areas with better lighting.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Manage the Minions">
    Deep floor bosses frequently summon lesser demons. Use your primary weapon to clear these adds quickly to prevent being swarmed while focusing on the main target.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Exploit Environmental Weaknesses">
    Some arenas contain explosive barrels or light traps. Luring the boss into these areas can deal massive damage and stun them for a duration.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Final Phase">
    The last 20% of a deep floor boss's health is the most dangerous. Use your "Ultimate" or strongest secondary weapons to bypass this phase as quickly as possible.
  </Step>
</Steps>
