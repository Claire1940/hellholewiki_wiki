<Section icon={Skull} title="Understanding Boss Mechanics in Hellhole">

In the demonic underworld of Hellhole, bosses serve as the ultimate gatekeepers to deeper floors. These high-tier entities are significantly more aggressive than standard hostile creatures, utilizing complex attack patterns that require precise timing and environmental awareness. Success in these encounters depends on your ability to recognize telegraphs—visual or auditory cues that precede a major attack.

Survival in Hellhole is dictated by the mantra "The only way out is down," meaning every boss encounter is a necessary hurdle for progression. Unlike regular enemies, bosses often have multiple health phases, each introducing new hazards or increasing the speed of their existing maneuvers.

| Boss Phase | Difficulty | Behavior Change | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phase 1** | Moderate | Standard telegraphs, slower projectiles | Observe patterns and save ammo |
| **Phase 2** | High | Increased movement speed, new AoE attacks | Prioritize dodging over sustained fire |
| **Final Stand** | Very High | Enraged state, constant aggression | Use all remaining resources/upgrades |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Bosses in Hellhole do not have a "leash" limit. Once a fight begins, the boss will pursue you across the entire floor area until either you descend or the entity is neutralized.

<Section icon={Target} title="Core Combat and Survival Tactics">

The actual combat loop in Hellhole requires a mix of aggression and patience. Over-extending to deal damage is the most common cause of death. Instead, adopt a "rhythmic combat" approach: dodge the boss's sequence, fire a controlled burst, and reposition immediately.

| Tactic | Execution | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Circle Strafing** | Move in a constant arc around the boss | Evades most linear projectiles |
| **Cover Peeking** | Use pillars to block line-of-sight | Forces the boss to reset animations |
| **Burst Fire** | Shoot in 3-5 round intervals | Maintains accuracy and ammo control |
| **Kiting** | Lead the boss into environmental traps | Deals massive passive damage |

Managing your distance is essential. If you stay too far, some bosses may trigger a "Leap" or "Teleport" attack that is harder to dodge than their standard projectiles. If you stay too close, you risk taking massive melee damage or being caught in a grab mechanic.

<Callout type="warning" title="Hazard Alert">
Deep-floor bosses often leave "Hellfire" trails on the ground. Standing in these areas deals damage over time (DoT) and reduces your movement speed by 30%.
</Callout>

</Section>
