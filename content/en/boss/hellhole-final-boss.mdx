<Section icon={Target} title="Final Boss Mechanics and Phases">

The **hellhole final boss** fight is structured into multiple distinct phases, each increasing in complexity. Understanding the visual cues for these attacks is the difference between a successful run and a quick trip back to the lobby. The boss utilizes a mix of physical strikes, demonic projectiles, and environmental manipulation.

During the initial phase, the boss remains relatively stationary, relying on long-range slams and summoned minions. As its health drops, it becomes significantly more mobile, eventually covering the arena in "Hellfire" which limits your movement options.

  <Step num="1" title="Observation Phase">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Minion Summoning">
    The boss retreats into the shadows and spawns elite demons. Switch to high-capacity weapons to clear the adds quickly before the boss returns.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enrage State">

  <Step num="4" title="The Final Stand">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Survival Strategies">

Surviving the **hellhole final boss** isn't just about shooting; it's about smart movement and environmental awareness. The arena is designed to trap players who stand still for too long. Utilizing the verticality of the environment and the limited cover available is essential for high-level play.

Experienced players often use a "circular strafe" technique, keeping the boss in the center of the arena while rotating along the outer edge. This minimizes the chance of being hit by the boss's primary frontal attacks while keeping the exit path clear.

| Strategy Component | Importance | Execution Detail |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stamina Management** | Critical | Never let your bar hit zero; keep a reserve for emergency dashes. |
| **Cover Utilization** | High | Use the stone pillars to block projectiles, but move before they crumble. |
| **Team Positioning** | Medium | Spread out to ensure the boss can only target one player at a time. |
| **Ammo Conservation** | High | Use secondary weapons for minions; save primary ammo for the boss. |
