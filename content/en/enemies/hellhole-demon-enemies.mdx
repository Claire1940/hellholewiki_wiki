<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Birth & The Scar">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Seven Sinners">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival Tactics and Countermeasures">

To survive against the **hellhole demon enemies**, one must understand the tools at their disposal. While the militia provides basic gear, the monastery’s supernatural influence can render traditional weapons less effective.

**Essential Survival Items:**
- **Flashlight**: Critical for navigating the dark corridors and finding hidden compartments.
- **Handgun**: Effective against individual monks, but ammo is extremely limited.
- **Newspaper Clippings**: Useful for tracking the "Missing Women" case and identifying potential ritual targets.
- **Ancient Texts**: Necessary for understanding the Latin prayers used to either facilitate or hinder the ritual.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Final Evolution: Baphomet">

The ultimate form among the **hellhole demon enemies** is the horned sabbatical goat, Baphomet. This entity represents the end of the world as we know it. Once Merrick completes the transformation, the gameplay shifts from survival-horror to a cosmic horror event.

**Baphomet's Abilities:**
- **Levitation**: Controls the bodies of everyone in the vicinity, posing them as inverted crosses.
- **Fly Swarm**: Can dissolve and reform using millions of flies, making physical attacks useless.
- **Reality Warping**: Causes barren trees to sprout and the sky to shatter, signifying the "Hellhole" state of Earth.
- **Gravity Manipulation**: Flips the entire chapel and its inhabitants upside down to mark his arrival.
