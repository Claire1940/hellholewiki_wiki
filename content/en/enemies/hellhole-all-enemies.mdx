<Section icon={Swords} title="Standard Enemy Types and Behaviors">

Most encounters in Hellhole consist of "trash mobs" that serve to chip away at your health before you reach the floor boss. While individually weak, their strength lies in numbers and environmental synergy. For example, a **Charred Zombie** is slow, but when paired with a **Spitter Skeleton**, you may find your movement restricted by acid pools, making you an easy target for the melee horde.

| Enemy Name | Primary Attack | Danger Level | Movement Speed | Special Traits |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Charred Zombie** | Melee Swipe | Low | Slow | High health, basic fodder |
| **Spitter Skeleton** | Acid Projectile | Medium | Moderate | Leaves poison pools on ground |
| **Gremlin** | Rapid Melee | High | Very Fast | Small hitbox, difficult to track |
| **Skeleton Archer** | Ranged Arrow | Medium | Moderate | High accuracy, stays at range |
| **Frozen Variant** | Chilling Touch | Medium | Slow | Can slow player movement speed |
| **Withered One** | Life Drain | High | Moderate | Found in Ancient Tomb floors |

<Callout type="warning" title="Acid Hazard">
The poison pools left by **Spitter Skeletons** deal damage over time. In narrow corridors or small treasure rooms, these can quickly end a run if you do not have enough **Speed Boost** upgrades to maneuver around them.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Boss Encounter: The Arena Guide">

The climax of a successful descent is the boss fight, usually occurring in a small, enclosed arena. This encounter scales based on your party size. A solo player will face a boss with approximately 4,000 HP, whereas a full crew will face a much larger health pool. The primary challenge is not just the boss's health, but the lack of space to avoid their area-of-effect (AoE) attacks.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Pre-Boss Preparation">
    Visit the final shop before the arena. Prioritize **Max HP** and **Speed Boosts**. If you have enough gears, purchase **Fire Rate** or **Accuracy** upgrades to maximize your DPS in the confined space.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Constant Mobility">
    The boss frequently spits poison or launches projectiles that cover the ground. Never stand still. Circle the perimeter of the arena to keep the boss centered while staying clear of the hazards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Magazine Management">
    With a solo HP pool of 4,000, you will need multiple reloads. Use the **Reload Heals** perk to recover 5 HP every time you swap mags, ensuring you stay topped off even if you take a stray hit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute and Descend">
    Once the boss reaches 25% health, they may enter a frantic state. Focus all fire on the head for critical damage (especially if you have the **Deadly Crits** upgrade) to finish the fight quickly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Scaling Mechanics">
Boss health scales dynamically. If teammates leave mid-run, the boss health may remain at the higher party scaling, making soloing significantly more difficult. Ensure your team is committed before entering the final descent.
</Callout>

</Section>
