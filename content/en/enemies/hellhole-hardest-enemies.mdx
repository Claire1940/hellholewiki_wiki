Surviving the **hellhole hardest enemies** is not just about aim; it is about the path you take and how you prepare for the next floor. The "only way out is down" motto emphasizes that you cannot retreat once a floor transition begins.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Scout the Perimeter">
    Before engaging any group, identify exits and health kit spawns. Never trigger a combat encounter in a dead-end room.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize the Screamer">
    If an Echo Screamer is present, it must die first. Its ability to call reinforcements can turn a manageable fight into a wipe in seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage the Reload Loop">
    Coordinate reloads with your movement. Use obstacles to break line-of-sight while swapping magazines to avoid being caught mid-animation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Elite Takedowns">
    Use grenades or heavy ordnance to strip the armor from Elites before finishing them with high-RPM primary weapons.
  </Step>
</Steps>
