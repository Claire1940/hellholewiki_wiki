<Section icon={Target} title="A & B Tier: Reliable Alternatives">

Not every player has immediate access to S-Tier gear. The A and B tiers of our **hellhole weapon tier list** feature weapons that are highly effective when used correctly, often specializing in specific niches that S-Tier weapons might overlook.

| Weapon | Tier | Range | Best Use Case |

| **Kar98k** | B | Long | High-skill ceiling sniping |

A-Tier weapons are often preferred by specialists. For example, the **M1 Garand** offers superior two-tap potential at distances where an SMG would simply tickle the opponent. Meanwhile, the **PPSH-41** provides even higher fire rates than the PPD40 but suffers from more erratic recoil, requiring a skilled hand to tame.

B-Tier weapons are generally considered "workhorse" guns. They are reliable, cheap to maintain, and effective in the early to mid-game. The **MP40**, for instance, has very predictable vertical recoil, making it a great tool for learning headshot tracking before moving up to more complex firearms.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">

</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimizing Your Loadout">

Simply picking a weapon from the top of the **hellhole weapon tier list** isn't enough. You must optimize your loadout to match your playstyle and the specific demands of the map. This involves balancing weight, ammo count, and utility items.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Base Weapon">
    Choose a weapon based on the map size. For urban hellholes, pick an SMG. For open fields, a semi-auto rifle is better.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Apply Performance Mods">
    Focus on stability and fire rate. Adding a heavy bolt or a polished trigger can significantly decrease your TTK.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Balance Your Weight">
    Ensure your total loadout weight doesn't penalize your stamina. High mobility is vital for escaping bad situations.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Field Testing">
    Take your build into a low-stakes skirmish to test the recoil pattern and ammo consumption before entering ranked play.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Weight Matters">
Overloading your character with too many grenades or secondary weapons will slow your sprint speed, making you an easy target for S-Tier SMG users.
</Callout>

</Section>
