### Movement and Positioning
Never stand still. The AI in Hellhole is designed to flank players who remain in cover for too long. Use a "Constant Retreat" tactic: fire a few rounds, move back to a choke point, and repeat. This forces the demons into a narrow line, making your shots much more efficient.

### Resource Management
Ammunition is scarce. You should always have a "Trash Killer" weapon and a "Boss Killer" weapon. Using your high-tier AR rounds on a single low-level zombie is a waste of resources that you will regret when the floor boss appears.

<Callout type="info" title="Expert Advice">
Listen for the audio cues. Every monster in Hellhole has a distinct "pre-attack" sound. Learning these sounds allows you to dodge without even seeing the enemy.
</Callout>
