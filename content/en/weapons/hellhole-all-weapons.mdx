### S-Tier Weapons

The **Photon Beam** represents the pinnacle of single-target DPS. It fires a continuous stream of high-energy particles that melt through elite enemies and bosses in seconds. However, its high energy consumption means you must save it for critical encounters rather than general stage clearing.

The **Plasma Cannon** is your primary tool for crowd control. It fires slow-moving, high-impact plasma spheres that detonate upon contact, dealing massive splash damage to clustered enemies. It is highly efficient for clearing narrow corridors.

### A-Tier Weapons

The **Laser Rifle** offers excellent precision and range. Its high projectile speed makes it highly reliable against fast-moving aerial targets. While it lacks splash damage, its armor-penetrating capabilities make it a solid choice against shielded ground units.

The **Spread Shot** fires multiple projectiles in a cone pattern. This weapon is ideal for defensive play, allowing you to clear incoming projectiles and small enemies over a wide area without needing precise aim.

### B-Tier Weapons

The **Standard Blaster** is your default armament. It has infinite ammunition, ensuring you are never completely defenseless. However, its low damage output makes it unsuitable for late-game stages and boss encounters.
