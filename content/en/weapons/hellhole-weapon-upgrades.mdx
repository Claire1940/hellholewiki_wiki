<Section icon={Zap} title="In-Run Hellhole Weapon Upgrades and RNG Mechanics">

Once you descend into the Magma Core, your focus shifts to **hellhole weapon upgrades** that appear dynamically during the run. These upgrades are found in specific "Upgrade Rooms" and offer a choice between three randomized buffs. Understanding the synergy between these buffs is the difference between a failed run and reaching the Crystal floor.

Upgrades are categorized by rarity: Common, Rare, and Epic. An Epic Damage upgrade can provide a massive 16% boost, which scales significantly as you stack it with Critical damage modifiers.

| Upgrade Name | Effect Description | Rarity Tier |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Deadly Crits** | Increases Critical Hit chance by 25% | Rare |
| **Heavy Rounds** | Increases raw base damage output | Common/Rare |
| **Adrenaline** | Boosts reload speed after a kill | Common |
| **Epic Damage** | Massive +16% boost to all damage | Epic |
| **Extended Mag** | Adds +5 rounds to the current magazine | Common |
| **Rapid Fire** | Increases Fire Rate (RPM) by 12% | Rare |

<Callout type="tip" title="Synergy Strategy">
If you are using the default pistol, prioritize **Magazine Size** and **Reload Speed**. Since the pistol has a small base magazine, increasing it to 17 or 22 rounds allows you to trigger "on-reload" perks less frequently but stay in the fight longer during Gremlin swarms.
</Callout>

When choosing your path, always check the room icons. If you see an Upgrade icon and a Treasure room icon together, prioritize the upgrade first to ensure you are strong enough to clear the subsequent combat wave.

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Maximizing Gear Earnings">

To afford the best **hellhole weapon upgrades**, you need a steady stream of Gears. Gears are the primary currency used in the main menu shop, and they are earned primarily through performance and exploration.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Clear Treasure Rooms">
    Always look for the golden and silver chests hidden in side rooms. These provide both immediate Gold for the run and Gears for your permanent account.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Complete Daily Missions">
    Check the bottom left of the lobby screen for your daily tasks. These often reward 100-500 Gears upon completion and reset every 24 hours.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reach Deeper Floors">
    The amount of Gears rewarded scales with the depth of your run. Reaching the Ancient Tomb or the Crystal floor yields significantly more currency than dying in the Magma Core.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat Bosses">
    Bosses drop a large cache of Gears. Even if you die shortly after a boss fight, the currency earned from that kill is permanently added to your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Currency Source | Average Yield | Difficulty |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Silver Chests** | 10-20 Gears | Very Easy |
| **Golden Chests** | 50-100 Gears | Easy |
| **Daily Quests** | 200+ Gears | Medium |
| **Floor Bosses** | 500+ Gears | Hard |

<Callout type="success" title="Economy Tip">
If you are struggling to earn Gears, focus on "Speedruns" to floor 3. The difficulty is low, and you can quickly gather chests and reset to maximize your hourly Gear income.
</Callout>

</Section>

While exploring the various layers of the pit, you will encounter NPC shops. These shops use the Gold you collect *during that specific run*. Unlike Gears, Gold is lost once the run ends, so you should spend it liberally to ensure you survive the next floor.
