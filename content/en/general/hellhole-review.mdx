<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Analyzing the Shocking Ending">

The final 20 minutes of *Hellhole* are what truly set it apart from other Netflix horror offerings. While the first two acts are a mixed bag of cliches and obvious turns, the ending escalates to an outlandish level that leaves many viewers stunned. It shifts from a grounded, albeit dark, thriller into a full-scale supernatural event.

The quality of the scenes in this final collection is significantly higher than the rest of the film. The atmosphere becomes truly claustrophobic, and the visual effects reach a peak that justifies the prior slow buildup. It is a risky move that pays off by giving the film a lasting impression that a standard "cult thriller" would not have achieved.
